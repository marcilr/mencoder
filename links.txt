Links
=====
MEncoder is a free command line transcoding tool released under the
GNU General Public License. It is a sibling of MPlayer, and can convert
all the formats that MPlayer understands into a variety of compressed
and uncompressed formats using different codecs.[1]
[1] MPlayer and MEncoder Status of codecs support, Retrieved on 2009-07-19
http://www.mplayerhq.hu/DOCS/codecs-status.html
